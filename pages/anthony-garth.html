Director

Lorenzo De Guia

Lorenzo is a Filipino-American writer and film director, originally from Southern California, now based out of Brooklyn and Los Angeles. By adopting a story-first approach, Lorenzo has built bold, emotional, and visually rich narratives filled with humanity and heart. Each world he crafts is imbued with realism, fostering a deep sense of empathy for the story. Ultimately, his goal is to create something that everyone involved is proud to have been a part of. He has worked with a wide range of clients including 3M, Nike, the NHL, General Mills, BMW, Goldman Sachs, as well as celebrities and athletes like LeBron James, Steph Curry, Mark Ronson, and more.
